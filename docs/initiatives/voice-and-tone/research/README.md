# Voice & Tone Research

## Iterations

### Iteration 1 — 2026-03-13: Landscape Survey
Five vectors covering voice/tone framework patterns, AI consulting language landscape, B2B persona frameworks, messaging frameworks (StoryBrand vs. alternatives), and content templates/editorial systems.

**Key finding:** Sherpa's "warm competence" voice occupies a genuinely empty lane. The strongest differentiator is structural ("we use what we ship"), not tonal. Don't formalize StoryBrand; use a layered hybrid (Dunford positioning + Raskin strategic narrative + JTBD per-persona messaging).

**Structural gaps identified:** Accessibility, inclusive language, AI agent voice, component-level content patterns, grammar/mechanics, readability target.

## Open Questions

3. How should the strategic narrative (Raskin model) be structured for Sherpa's "agentic governance gap"?
