# Vector 2: Startup Funding & Traction in Agent Governance

**Question:** Which startups have raised funding specifically for AI agent governance, and what traction signals exist in the market as of early 2026?
**Agent dispatched:** 2026-03-17

## Findings

### Known Vendors — Detailed Profiles

**Lakera** (Zurich, founded 2021)
- Total funding: ~$30M across 2 rounds. $20M Series A (Jul 2024) led by Atomico with Citi Ventures, Dropbox, redalpine. Revenue reached $5.7M in 2025 with 52 employees.
- **Acquired by Check Point Software for ~$300M** in Q4 2025. Now forms Check Point's Global Center of Excellence for AI Security. Products Lakera Red (red teaming) and Lakera Guard (runtime enforcement) integrated into Check Point Infinity Platform.
- Pivot trajectory: LLM security (prompt injection) → full agentic AI security. Acquisition validates the category — cybersecurity incumbents buying at 10x revenue multiples.

**Arize AI** (San Francisco, founded 2020)
- Total funding: $131M across 4 rounds. $70M Series C (Feb 2025) led by Adams Street Partners, with M12 (Microsoft), Datadog, PagerDuty, Foundation Capital, Battery Ventures, TCV.
- Product: Arize AX platform with agent-specific observability — traces, evals, "Agent Pathing" for execution route analysis. Integrations with Autogen, LangGraph, OpenAI Agents, smolagents. PagerDuty partnership.
- Pivot trajectory: MLOps observability (tabular ML) → LLM observability → agent observability.

**Zenity** (Tel Aviv, founded 2020)
- Total funding: $59.5M across 4 rounds. $38M Series B (Oct 2024) co-led by Third Point Ventures and DTCP, with M12 (Microsoft), Intel Capital, Vertex Ventures.
- **Named Gartner Cool Vendor in Agentic AI TRiSM** (2025). Deep Microsoft integration — inline prevention for Copilot Studio agents, securing tool invocations including MCP servers. Fortune 500 customer base.
- Pivot trajectory: Low-code security → AI agent security/governance. One of the most credible pure-play agent governance vendors.

**Credo AI** (San Francisco, founded 2020)
- Total funding: $41.3M. Latest: $21M (Jul 2024) from CrimsoNox Capital, Mozilla Ventures, FPV Ventures, with Sands Capital, Decibel VC, Booz Allen Hamilton. **Valuation: $101M** (2x previous). Revenue tripled in 2024.
- Customers: Mastercard, Cisco, PepsiCo, Northrop Grumman, Booz Allen, Databricks. Available on Microsoft Marketplace. **Named in Gartner's 2025 Market Guide for AI Governance Platforms.** Launched Advisory Services Aug 2025.
- Pivot trajectory: Responsible AI compliance → full AI governance platform with agent-specific lifecycle management. Presented at Davos 2026.

**Holistic AI** (London, founded 2020)
- Funding: Undisclosed total (likely $10-20M range). Investors include Tola Capital, Mozilla Ventures, Premji Invest, Dallas Venture Capital. Also received UK government grant ~$600K.
- Customers: Unilever, Siemens, Mapfre, Michelin, other Fortune 500s. 79 employees.

**Patronus AI** (San Francisco, founded 2023)
- Total funding: $40.1M across 3 rounds. $17M Series A (May 2024) led by Notable Capital, with Lightspeed Venture Partners, Datadog, Factorial Capital.
- Product: LLM evaluation and guardrails. Lynx hallucination detection model outperforms GPT-4o on RAG accuracy. Self-serve API. Customers: AngelList, Pearson, HP. Recently launched ORSI environments for agent self-improvement.
- Pivot trajectory: LLM evaluation → agent guardrails and RL training environments.

**Galileo** (San Francisco, founded 2021)
- Total funding: $68M. $45M Series B (Oct 2024) led by Scale Venture Partners, with Premji Invest, Databricks Ventures, ServiceNow Ventures, Citi Ventures, SentinelOne Ventures. **834% revenue growth in 2024.**
- Product: AI reliability platform with proprietary Luna evaluation models (sub-200ms latency). Customers: HP, Twilio, Reddit, Comcast.
- Pivot trajectory: Data quality for ML → LLM evaluation → agent observability and guardrails.

**Arthur AI** (New York, founded 2018)
- Total funding: $63M. $42M Series B (Sep 2022).
- **Launched Agent Discovery & Governance (ADG) platform in Dec 2025** — first dedicated enterprise platform for discovering and governing agentic systems. Available on Google Cloud Marketplace (Jan 2026). Safeguarded 1B+ tokens in 2025.
- Pivot trajectory: ML model monitoring → LLM guardrails → full agent discovery and governance.

**OneTrust** (Atlanta, founded 2016)
- Total funding: $1.13B across 7 rounds. Last round: $150M (Jul 2023) at $4.5B valuation. Exploring PE sale at ~$10B+ (Nov 2025).
- **Expanded platform with real-time AI governance and agent oversight** (Mar 2026). Agent detection and inventory, programmatic guardrails, continuous monitoring.
- Pivot trajectory: Privacy compliance (GDPR) → data governance → AI governance → agent governance.

**AvePoint** (Jersey City, NASDAQ: AVPT, founded 2001)
- Public company. Revenue $419.5M (FY2025), profitable. 2026 guidance: $509-517M.
- **Launched AgentPulse** — centralized command for tracking, governing, controlling AI agents across Microsoft and Google. Feb 2026 expansion.
- Pivot trajectory: SharePoint management → M365 data governance → AI/agent governance.

### New Entrants (2025-2026 Vintage)

**JetStream Security** (founded 2025, launched Mar 2026)
- **$34M seed** led by Redpoint Ventures with CrowdStrike Falcon Fund. Angels: George Kurtz (CrowdStrike CEO), Assaf Rappaport (Wiz CEO), Frederic Kerrest (Okta co-founder).
- Founded by Raj Rajamani, former CPO of CrowdStrike. AI asset mapping across clouds, SaaS, endpoints. MCP server sprawl and key sprawl management.

**Defakto** (formerly SPIRL, founded ~2022)
- **$30.75M Series B** (Oct 2025) led by XYZ Venture Capital. Total raised ~$50M.
- First complete Non-Human Identity and Access Management Platform — securing identities for AI agents, services, pipelines.

**t54 Labs** (San Francisco, founded Jan 2025)
- **$5M seed** led by Anagram, PL Capital, Franklin Templeton with Ripple, Virtuals Ventures.
- Identity, verification, and compliance tools for AI agents that transact independently. Blockchain-based settlement layer.

**Mindgard** (Lancaster UK, university spin-off)
- $11.9M total, including $8M round (Dec 2024) led by .406 Ventures.

**Monitaur** (Austin, founded ~2019)
- $13.2M total. $6M Series A (May 2024) led by Cultivation Capital.
- Model governance for regulated industries. Named in Gartner's 2025 AI TRiSM Market Guide.

## Implications

1. **The governance category is real and well-funded.** Combined funding across catalogued vendors exceeds $1.5B. The claim "no governance tooling exists" from earlier initiatives is definitively false for enterprise. However, no vendor targets small-team/solo-operator convention-based governance.
2. **Acquisitions confirm category value but suggest transitional standalone form.** $2.6B+ in governance acquisitions in 2024-2025. Incumbents buy rather than build. Standalone governance vendors may be absorbed into security/infrastructure platforms.
3. **The gap is developer-facing, convention-based governance.** Every funded vendor targets enterprise buyers. Sherpa's filesystem-based approach has no funded competitor.
4. **Agent discovery is the emerging battleground.** Arthur AI, JetStream, OneTrust all launched discovery capabilities in late 2025.
5. **Microsoft ecosystem dominance.** Zenity, AvePoint, Credo AI, OneTrust all deeply integrated. M12 invested in Zenity and Arize.
