**Relevance:** Directly informs website voice — the site must balance consulting pitch with authentic career narrative

## Key Insights for Voice Discovery

1. **"Proof-of-methodology" framing** — Sherpa/WavePoint positioned as "I built this to prove it works" rather than "I founded a consulting company"
2. **13-year engineering history is the credibility signal** — not stats about AI failure rates. Staff Engineer, 6 years at PartySlate, first developer at a startup, design system lead.
3. **Chapter, not identity** — founding is a deliberate chapter of applied learning, not a permanent identity shift
4. **Multiple audiences** — the website serves potential consulting clients AND the broader professional ecosystem (employers, collaborators, community)
5. **"I've done the solo version. Now I want the multiplied one"** — the honesty about where you are IS the voice

## Implications for Homepage

- Lead with engineering practitioner credibility, not company positioning
- The founding story is about building something to prove it works, not about starting a business
- The 13yr→founder→next chapter narrative is more honest and compelling than "we are an AI governance consultancy"
