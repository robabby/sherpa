# Agentic Consulting Landscape — Research

## Summary

Landscape survey of solo operators and micro-teams running AI agent-powered consulting businesses in 2025-2026. Covers who's doing it, business models, unit economics, quality realities, trust dynamics, client acquisition, and legal/insurance risks.

## Key Findings (Iteration 1)

- Unit economics are proven: $25K-$40K/month at 70-85% margins for solo+agents
- Nobody has governance tooling — everyone uses ad-hoc markdown + manual review
- Outcome-based pricing preferred by 73% of clients
- AI output requires significant human QA (1.7x code defects, Deloitte hallucination scandals)
- Twitter/X build-in-public is the proven solo acquisition channel
- Insurance industry adding AI exclusions; 73 new state AI laws in 2025
