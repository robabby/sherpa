# Vector 5: Grant Readiness Requirements

**Question:** What documentation and registrations does a small consulting LLC need before applying for grants?
**Agent dispatched:** 2026-03-18

## Findings

### Federal Registration Chain (Sequential — Must Do In Order)

1. **EIN** — Required for everything. Verify IRS CP 575 confirmation letter exists. If lost, request IRS Letter 147C.
2. **SAM.gov (UEI)** — Replaced DUNS as of April 2022. 12-character alphanumeric ID. Takes 7-10 business days. Requires:
   - Login.gov account
   - EIN (must match IRS records exactly)
   - Physical business address (no P.O. Box)
   - Banking info (routing + account number)
   - NAICS codes
   - **Notarized Entity Administrator Letter** (most common rejection reason — use GSA template, remote notarization accepted)
   - Two administrators to prevent lockout
   - **Must renew every 365 days** or registration lapses
3. **Grants.gov** — Register after SAM.gov is active. Same-day process. Use same email as SAM.gov EBiz POC.

### NAICS Codes for AI Consulting

- **Primary: 541519** (Other Computer Related Services) — 45% of federal AI contracts. Size standard: 150 employees.
- **Secondary: 541611** (Administrative Management and General Management Consulting) — IT strategy, digital transformation. Size standard: $24.5M revenue.
- **Also relevant: 541690** (Other Scientific/Technical Consulting) — $19M revenue standard.
- **For SBIR: 541715** (R&D in Physical/Engineering/Life Sciences).
- Sherpa easily qualifies as "small" under all codes.

### SBA — No Direct Grants

SBA explicitly states it does not provide grants for starting/expanding a business. SBA grants go to nonprofits, community orgs, and educational institutions. Exception: SBIR/STTR R&D grants.

### Common Documents Required

- Business plan (10-15 pages: executive summary, market analysis, operational plan, financial plan)
- 2-3 years of federal tax returns (personal + business)
- Profit & loss statements (2-3 years or since inception)
- Current balance sheet
- Cash flow analysis and financial projections
- Articles of Organization / LLC Operating Agreement
- Business licenses (WA State UBI, City of Bellingham endorsement)
- EIN confirmation letter (CP 575)
- Bank account information (EFT/direct deposit)
- Letters of support or reference (for competitive grants)

### Washington State Certifications

- **OMWBE Certification** — WA Office of Minority and Women's Business Enterprises. Requires 51% qualifying ownership, personal net worth under $2.047M, gross receipts under $31.84M. ~60 days processing. Opens state/local contracting advantages.

### Bellingham-Specific

- **SBDC at WWU** (2219 Rimland Drive, Suite 319, Bellingham) — Free advising on grant readiness. First call to make.

## Practical Prioritized Checklist

### Tier 1: Do Immediately (Week 1-2) — Gates Everything

1. Confirm EIN status + CP 575 letter
2. Confirm WA State UBI + Bellingham city endorsement are current
3. Select NAICS codes (recommend primary 541519 or 541611)
4. Register on SAM.gov (7-10 day lead time, notarized letter required)
5. Register on Grants.gov (after SAM.gov active)

### Tier 2: Financial Readiness (Week 1-3)

6. Gather 2-3 years of tax returns
7. Prepare P&L statements and current balance sheet
8. Create 12-24 month cash flow projections
9. Write/update business plan (10-15 pages)
10. Collect legal docs in one folder (Articles, Operating Agreement, EIN, licenses)

### Tier 3: Strategic Positioning (Week 2-4)

11. Contact Bellingham SBDC for free advising session
12. Evaluate OMWBE certification if applicable
13. Create SBIR.gov account, monitor NSF AI solicitations
14. Monitor HB 1833 (Spark Act) status
15. Set SAM.gov renewal calendar reminder (365 days)
