# Research — wa-business-grants

## Summary

Iteration 1 surveyed the full non-dilutive funding landscape across 5 vectors: WA state programs, federal grants, Bellingham/Whatcom local resources, AI/tech foundations, and grant readiness requirements.

**Key finding:** WA state and local grants are structurally misaligned with a small AI consulting LLC. The strongest opportunities are federal (NSF PESOSE, SBIR) and open-source ecosystem (NLnet, Sovereign Tech Fund). Bellingham provides excellent free advisory resources (SBDC, SCORE, NWIRC) that should be leveraged for application preparation.
