# Vector 5: Consulting Pricing for AI Infrastructure Deployment

**Question:** What does the market bear for managed AI infrastructure deployment as a consulting service? What do comparable services charge — fractional CTO, DevOps-as-a-Service, AI/ML consulting? What pricing models work?
**Agent dispatched:** 2026-03-16

## Findings

### Comparable Service Pricing Benchmarks

**Fractional CTO**

- Monthly retainers: $3,000-$15,000/month. North America: $10,000-$16,000/month; Western Europe: $5,000-$12,000/month. ([TLVTech](https://www.tlvtech.io/post/understanding-fractional-cto-rates-a-guide-for-entrepreneurs-and-business-leaders), [Fractional CTO Experts](https://fractionalctoexperts.com/blog/complete-guide-fractional-cto-services-pricing-2025))
- Hourly: $150-$500/hour, AI/ML specialists command 30%+ premiums. ([Alberto Sadde](https://albertosadde.com/fractional-cto))
- Full-time CTO costs ~$487K/year all-in — fractional positions at 10-30% for equivalent strategic value. ([CTOx](https://ctox.com/roi-of-fractional-tech-leadership-a-data-analysis/))

**DevOps-as-a-Service**

- Retainers: $2,000-$6,000/month basic, $5,000-$30,000/month comprehensive. ([SDH Global](https://sdh.global/services/devops-services/), [SHALB](https://shalb.com/prices/))
- Hourly: $125-$300/hour US firms. Mid-tier $60-100/hour; premium $150-199/hour. ([Dataconomy](https://dataconomy.com/2025/11/05/devops-service-costs-in-america-what-5-top-us-companies-charge/))
- Project-based: under $25K small-scale, $25K-$50K+ complex. ([Techreviewer](https://techreviewer.co/pricing/devops-consulting))

**AI/ML Infrastructure Consulting**

- Daily rates (US): $600-$1,200/day independents, $1,500-$3,000/day senior, $2,500-$3,500+/day top-tier firms. ([Nicola Lazzari](https://nicolalazzari.ai/guides/ai-consultant-pricing-us))
- AI readiness assessments: $2,000-$8,000 fixed fee. ([Salt Technologies](https://www.salttechno.ai/services/ai-readiness-audit/), [Leanware](https://www.leanware.co/insights/how-much-does-an-ai-consultant-cost))
- AI implementation: $5,000-$15,000 for 1-2 agents; $10,000-$50,000 simple; $50,000-$200,000 medium complexity. ([Stack](https://stack.expert/blog/ai-consulting-proposals-that-close))
- AI retainers: Essential (5-10 hrs/mo) $2,000-$5,000/mo; Standard (10-25 hrs/mo) $5,000-$15,000/mo; Comprehensive (25+ hrs/mo) $15,000-$50,000/mo. ([Leanware](https://www.leanware.co/insights/how-much-does-an-ai-consultant-cost))
- SMBs spend $10,000-$50,000 on initial AI projects, with $10K-$15K common for small business. ([AI Essentials](https://aiessentials.us/blog/how-much-does-it-cost-to-hire-an-ai-consultant-for-my-small))

**AI Micro-Consulting (Solo/Independent)**

- $200-$500/hour equivalent for packaged deliverables. 48-hour competitive analysis commands $1,500-$3,000 because client pays for outcome. ([Digital Applied](https://www.digitalapplied.com/blog/ai-micro-consulting-premium-rates-solo-guide))
- Solo retainers: $2,000-$5,000/month light touch; $8,000-$15,000+/month intensive. ([Digital Applied](https://www.digitalapplied.com/blog/ai-micro-consulting-premium-rates-solo-guide))

**MSP Infrastructure Margins**

- Target gross margin: 50% on services, 25% on products/hardware. Sustainable floor: 50%+ overall. ([Kaseya](https://www.kaseya.com/resource/msp-pricing-managed-it-services-pricing/), [CloudBolt](https://www.cloudbolt.io/msp-best-practices/msp-profit-margins/))
- Per-user managed services: $50-$250/user/month. ([V2 Cloud](https://v2cloud.com/blog/how-to-price-your-msp-services))

**AI Literacy Training**

- Half-day workshops: $500-$1,500/person; full-day: $1,000-$3,000/person. ([SetupBots](https://setupbots.com/blog/ai-training-services-pricing-guide))
- Corporate team workshops: $5,000-$10,000 flat for half-day, small team. ([Futurist Speakers](https://www.futuristsspeakers.com/how-much-does-a-training-workshop-cost/))
- Corporate packages: $12,000-$250,000 total depending on scope. ([Bizzuka](https://www.bizzuka.com/how-much-does-ai-training-for-businesses-cost/))

### Pricing Models That Work

**Three-phase structure dominates:**

1. **Paid Discovery / Assessment** (fixed fee) — Short engagement producing written deliverable. $1,500-$5,000. ([NMS Consulting](https://nmsconsulting.com/consulting-fees-and-pricing-in-2026/))
2. **Fixed-Fee Implementation** — Build/deploy/configure with milestones. Scope from discovery, not hours. ([DEV Community](https://dev.to/wedgemethoddev/how-i-structure-every-ai-consulting-engagement-the-5-phase-framework-13f8))
3. **Monthly Retainer** — Ongoing support, optimization, advisory. Predictable recurring revenue. ([Consulting Success](https://www.consultingsuccess.com/consulting-retainer))

**Value-based pricing ascendant:** 73% of consulting clients prefer outcome-tied pricing. Formula: identify financial impact, price at 10-25% of value delivered. ([Agentive AIQ](https://agentiveaiq.com/blog/how-much-should-i-charge-for-ai-consulting-pricing-guide))

**Productized tiers for solo/small:** Three tiers (entry, standard, premium) provide comparison points, reduce sales friction. ([Assembly](https://assembly.com/blog/productized-services))

**AI pricing shifting:** Flat subscriptions giving way to hybrid (base + usage tiers). Every token/inference has real marginal cost, unlike traditional SaaS. ([Bessemer Venture Partners](https://www.bvp.com/atlas/the-ai-pricing-and-monetization-playbook))

## Implications

**Sherpa's pricing structure, market-informed:**

| Phase | Model | Range |
|-------|-------|-------|
| Discovery + Config + Deploy | Fixed fee | $2,500-$7,500 |
| Infrastructure hosting | Monthly, ~50% margin on cost | $50-$400/month |
| Convention tuning retainer | Monthly | $500-$2,000/month |
| AI literacy training | Per-workshop | $1,500-$3,000/workshop |

**Key insights:**

1. **The value is in configuration, confirmed.** AI readiness assessments ($2K-$8K) and agent deployments ($5K-$15K) are the market comparables. Sherpa's discovery produces a concrete config artifact, not a slide deck — this is a differentiator worth highlighting in pricing conversations.

2. **Infrastructure hosting margin is trivial alone.** At $5-$200/mo cost, 50% margin yields only $2.50-$100/mo. Hosting revenue only matters as part of the bundle (hosting + governance retainer). Consider rolling infrastructure into the retainer rather than billing separately.

3. **Three-tier productized menu is proven.** Entry (desktop-only, Sm tier), Standard (code dep + web, Md tier), Premium (all surfaces, Lg+ tier) — each tier maps to a deployment tier and a price tier. Reduces sales friction.

4. **Discovery as standalone product is validated.** Industry consensus: separate diagnosis from delivery. Even a 30-minute Shayna-style discovery has value — but pricing it at $200-$500 may not justify the sales overhead. Bundle for Sm, standalone for Lg+.

5. **Outcome-based pricing is the direction.** 73% preference validates Sherpa's approach. "Your team makes X better decisions per month" is more compelling than "you get Y hours of my time."

## Open Questions

1. Where does desktop-only (zero infra) deployment fit in pricing? 30-minute install has near-zero COGS. Loss-leader, or still carry a discovery fee ($1,500+ per market norms)?
2. Should convention tuning retainer be usage-based (active agents, tasks dispatched) or fixed monthly?
3. Price sensitivity for Sherpa's early clients (small businesses, solo operators) — may sit below the $2K/mo advisory floor. Trails could map to price tiers.
4. Software license vs services-only model? Open-source framework is free; value is in discovery + config + tuning. MSP model (services only) seems cleaner.
5. GPU inference margin: at $50-$200/mo cost, is it worth billing separately or rolled into retainer?
6. Renewal justification: 2026 is when "prove the ROI" hits. Sherpa needs outcome metrics (tasks dispatched, decisions improved) before first renewals.
