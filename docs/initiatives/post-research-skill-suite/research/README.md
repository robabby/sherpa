# Post-Research Skill Suite — Research

## Summary

Designing a suite of skills that slot into the product development pipeline between `/rr` (research/discovery) and `/plan-tasks` (execution dispatch). Informed by Anthropic's internal "docs to demos" process, the broader AI skills ecosystem, and novel AI-enabled product development activities.

## Iteration 1 (2026-03-13)

Surveyed three domains in parallel:
1. Anthropic's internal product process and skills
2. How advanced product teams integrate AI into their workflows
3. Novel product development activities uniquely enabled by AI

**Result:** Expanded the skill suite from 6 to 9 skills. Original six (shape, design, stake, spike, memo, radar) validated. Three new skills added from research (premortem, stress-test, retro). Decision journaling folded into existing skills as convention infrastructure.
