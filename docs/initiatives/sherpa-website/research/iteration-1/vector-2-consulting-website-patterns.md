# Vector 2: Consulting Website Patterns

**Question:** What do the best AI/tech consulting firm websites look like? What patterns build credibility with enterprise buyers?
**Agent dispatched:** 2026-03-13

## Findings

### Hero/Above-the-Fold Patterns

- **Problem-first, not product-first.** Highest-converting B2B hero sections lead with the business problem, not capabilities. ([thrivethemes.com](https://thrivethemes.com/hero-section-examples/))
- **Tagline structures from top firms:**
  - Palantir: "Foundational Software of Tomorrow. Delivered Today." — outcome + urgency
  - ThoughtWorks: "We don't just do AI. We do AI that works." — contrasts hype vs reality
  - Anthropic: "AI research and products that put safety at the frontier" — value-anchored
  - Scale AI: "Reliable AI Systems for the World's Most Important Decisions" — stakes + trust
  - Slalom: "Perspective is power" — abstract but immediately supported by concrete copy
- **Headlines under 10 words with the benefit obvious.** Avoid "empower," "unlock," "unleash." ([blueseedling.com](https://www.blueseedling.com/blog/how-i-learned-to-stop-worrying-and-love-the-tagline-why-and-how-to-write-an-unforgettable-tagline-for-your-b2b-startup/))
- **CTA patterns:** Primary CTA is always high-value/low-commitment. "Book a Demo," "Start Building," "Let's talk." Never "Learn More."
- **Layout:** Headline + subtext on left, product image or illustration on right, CTA near bottom-left. F-pattern scanning.

### Trust Signals

- **79% of B2B buyers rely on social proof.** Enterprise buying journeys involve 11-20 stakeholders. ([trajectorywebdesign.com](https://www.trajectorywebdesign.com/blog/b2b-website-trust-signals))
- **Eight trust signal categories** (priority order): design clarity/speed, testimonials with name/title/photo, highlighted results, client logos (8-12), accreditations, security certs, team visibility, authoritative content. ([webstacks.com](https://www.webstacks.com/blog/trust-signals))
- **75% of decision-makers trust brands affiliated with industry experts.** ([theinsightcollective.com](https://www.theinsightcollective.com/insights/why-b2b-buyers-trust-brands-partner-with-industry-experts))
- **Palantir clusters trust as third-party rankings:** Dresner #1, IDC #1, Forrester Wave Leader. Never self-declared.
- **Slalom uses award badges:** AWS GenAI Partner of the Year, Forbes Best Management Consulting.

### Case Study Formats

- **Palantir's "Impact Studies" are the gold standard:** 100% named clients (Wendy's, Citi, Heineken, Panasonic, United Airlines), executive quote with photo, 4-5 bulleted metrics, "Watch full keynote" CTA, filterable card grid. ([palantir.com/impact](https://palantir.com/impact/))
- **Challenge-solution-results is universal** but best firms add: implementation friction, human impact, unexpected benefits. ([velocitypartners.com](https://velocitypartners.com/blog/how-to-write-an-anonymous-case-study-that-doesnt-suck/))
- **Anonymous case studies can be powerful:** Use descriptive references ("Fortune 500 manufacturer" not "Bristol-based company"), lead with results, anonymity enables more honest storytelling. ([equinetmedia.com](https://www.equinetmedia.com/blog/how-to-write-a-case-study-anonymous-client), [atlanticbt.com](https://www.atlanticbt.com/insights/anonymous-case-studies/))

### Visual Design Language

- **Dark mode dominates AI/tech consulting.** 80%+ user preference. Dark = premium, focused, futuristic. ([colorhero.io](https://colorhero.io/blog/dark-mode-color-palettes-2025))
- **Color patterns:** Anthropic: dark + cream + terracotta (#d97757). ThoughtWorks: dark + burgundy/coral (#bd4257). BCG X: near-black + bright teal (#00FFCC). Scale AI: dark blue/black (#00000f).
- **Typography:** Sans-serif dominates. Custom fonts signal investment (BCG: Henderson BCG Sans, Scale AI: Aeonik). Large display headings.
- **Animation:** Anthropic uses extensive GSAP scroll triggers, word-by-word entrance. Palantir embeds Vimeo videos. Subtle micro-interactions enhance without overwhelming.

### Navigation/Information Architecture

- **2-7 main categories.** More overwhelms. ([nngroup.com](https://www.nngroup.com/articles/ia-vs-navigation/))
- **Observed patterns:**
  - Palantir (product-led): AIP | Foundry | Gotham | Apollo | Bootcamps | Start Building
  - ThoughtWorks (service-led): What we do | AI/works | Who we work with | Insights | Engineering | Careers
  - Anthropic (mission-led): Research | Economic Futures | Commitments | Learn | News | Try Claude
- **Dual-audience patterns:** HashiCorp separates developer.hashicorp.com from hashicorp.com. ThoughtWorks separates Engineering from What We Do.

### Blog/Content Integration

- **ThoughtWorks runs four publications** — Technology Radar, Perspectives, Decoder, Looking Glass. Each has own identity and audience.
- **Anthropic deeply integrates research with marketing.** No separation between "marketing blog" and "research."
- **Content hubs outperform simple blogs.** Topic-organized destinations compound SEO. ([galileotechmedia.com](https://galileotechmedia.com/using-content-hubs-for-thought-leadership/))

### Patterns That Build Enterprise Credibility

1. **Anti-hype positioning wins.** ThoughtWorks ("AI that works"), Slalom ("AI, hold the hype"). Enterprise buyers are exhausted by hype.
2. **Third-party validation > self-declared expertise.** Palantir leads with Dresner, IDC, Forrester.
3. **Branded methodology signals repeatability.** ThoughtWorks: AI/works. Kendall: Kendall Framework + Role Context Blocks.
4. **Education as trust-building funnel.** Palantir's AIP Bootcamps, Anthropic's Academy, Kendall's workshops.
5. **Outcome-based pricing eliminates objections.** 73% preference.
6. **Open-source framework IS the trust signal** for developer audience. HashiCorp: 100M+ Terraform downloads became the trust signal that closed Fortune 500 deals.
7. **Content hubs > chronological blogs.** Compound SEO + knowledge authority.
8. **Team visibility matters disproportionately for small firms.** Founder thought leadership is the primary trust engine without Fortune 500 logos.

## Implications

1. **Lead with the problem, not the product.** "91% of C-suite execs admit faking AI knowledge" as hero stat.
2. **Anti-hype positioning is Sherpa's natural lane.** Research data (95% pilot failure, Deloitte debacles) provides ammunition.
3. **The framework IS the trust signal** without Fortune 500 logos. GitHub adoption metrics become proxy for client logos.
4. **Content hubs over chronological blog.** Topic-organized research (AI Literacy, Agentic Workflows, Governance).
5. **Dark mode with warm accent.** Amber/gold for "sherpa" mountain guide warmth — distinctive from cold blue/teal.
6. **Founder visibility is non-negotiable.** Thought leadership through content, not credential claims.
7. **Start with anonymous case studies.** Use vulnerability as social proof, add named studies as they emerge.
8. **Education as conversion funnel.** AI literacy workshops as the primary CTA.
