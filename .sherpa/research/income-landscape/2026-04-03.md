### 🚨 Big News: AI-Powered Discovery Expansion
**Etsy listings are now appearing in:**
- **ChatGPT Instant Checkout experience**
- **Google Gemini & AI Mode in Google Search**

*Eligibility:* US sellers opted into Offsite Ads. This is massive for discovery — AI shopping assistants are routing buyers directly to Etsy listings. **Implication:** Listing copy quality and keyword precision matter more than ever since AI models are parsing listing text to match purchase intent.

### Review Rating Calculation Change
- Etsy is updating how shop ratings are calculated: **lifetime reviews with recency weighting**
- More recent reviews carry more weight (vs pure average)
- **Good news for new shops:** Good early reviews have more impact; a fresh shop can build reputation quickly
- **Risk for established shops:** Old bad reviews matter less, but recent ones hurt more

### New Seller Tool: Targeted Offer Performance
- Etsy added **performance metrics for targeted offers** directly in Sales & Discounts dashboard
- Shows: offers sent, purchases, conversion rate, revenue per offer
- Useful for measuring coupon/discount effectiveness at launch

### Stable Listing URLs
- Etsy improved URL stability for listings to **boost external SEO**
- Listings now maintain consistent URLs better, improving Google indexing
