# Airbnb Interview Prep — Staff Dev AI Engineer (March 27, 2026)

## Interview Format

**5 onsite rounds** (4-6 hours total):
1. **Coding round:** Data structures & algorithms (45-60 min)
2. **System design (LLD):** Low-level design
3. **System design (HLD):** High-level design
4. **1-2 Behavioral/Cultural rounds:** Values alignment, leadership examples

**Timeline:** 3-6 weeks from application to decision

**Staff-level (G9) emphasis:** Technical leadership, mentorship, cross-functional collaboration, architectural decision-making.
