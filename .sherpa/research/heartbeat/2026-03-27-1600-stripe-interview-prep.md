# Stripe Interview Prep — Staff/Design Engineer (March 27, 2026)

## Interview Format

**4-5 rounds:**
1. **Phone/Video Screen** — behavioral + technical screening
2. **Coding Round** — 60 min, practical problems (not pure algorithms)
3. **Integration Round** — unique to Stripe, API integration and real-world implementation. Major filter (~60% of otherwise strong candidates don't pass this)
4. **System Design** — payment-focused architecture questions
5. **Behavioral/Culture Fit** — leadership and decision-making scenarios

**Timeline:** 4-8 weeks total

## Staff Engineer Expectations

- Expected to have opinions on everyone's work despite no direct reports
- Drive technical direction without formal authority
- Handle production incidents and scope creep decisions
- Focus on collaboration, ownership, decision-making under ambiguity
- "Engineering-style code" — shallow hierarchies, clean architecture

## Design Engineer Role

Less documented than standard engineering roles. Likely involves:
- Design systems work (Stripe has strong design system infrastructure)
- Frontend implementation with design sensibilities
- Cross-functional collaboration between design and engineering

## Tech Stack

- **Backend:** Ruby on Rails (primary), Go, Java, Scala
- **Frontend:** React, TypeScript
- **Infrastructure:** Distributed systems
- **Interview languages:** Python, Java, C++, Go, Ruby (any mainstream)

## Culture

- **"Culture of writing"** — documentation and clear communication highly valued
- High technical standards, engineering excellence focus
- Collaborative with deep technical discussions
- High intensity, fast-paced
- Writing-heavy culture (memos, design docs, RFCs)
