# Vercel Interview Prep — Design Engineer (March 27, 2026)

## Interview Format

- **Timeline:** 3-5 weeks (90-day Glassdoor claim appears outdated)
- **Rounds:** 4-5 typical for senior technical roles
  1. Recruiter screen
  2. Technical phone/video screen
  3. Take-home project or technical deep dive
  4. Onsite (virtual) — system design, coding, cultural fit
  5. Final decision round

## What "Design Engineer" Means at Vercel

- **NOT traditional frontend engineer** — hybrid role: design sensibility + engineering execution
- "Responsible for the highest level of polish, creativity and interaction on Vercel"
- Embedded in product teams for features taking 1+ months to launch
- Bridges gap between pure design and pure engineering
- May own entire design-to-implementation pipeline (sometimes no dedicated designer)
- Pixel-perfect UI, interactive prototyping, animation, component library work
